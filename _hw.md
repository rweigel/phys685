# Delta Functions

1. Suppose $I = \int_{-2}^{2}g(x)dx$. Compute $\int_{-1}^{1}g(2x)dx$ and $\int_{-1}^{1}g(-2x)dx$ in terms of $I$. Show graphically why the result makes sense
using $g(x)=x^2$.

2. Show that $\int \delta(bx)f(x)dx = \frac{f(0)}{|b|}$.

3. Find $\int \delta(a + bx)f(x)dx$.

# Divergence Theorem

   when the volume of integration $\mathcal{V}$ is a sphere centered on the origin with

   1. radius b/2
